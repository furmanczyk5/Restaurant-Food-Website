Moroccan Chicken Salad

A flavorful main dish salad featuring chicken and rice.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup cooked rice
  • 1/2 cup shredded cooked chicken
  • 1/2 cup shredded carrots
  • 1 tablespoon sliced scallions
  • 1 tablespoon chopped Kalamata olives
  • 1 tablespoon reduced-sodium chicken broth
  • 1 tablespoon orange juice
  • 1/2 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1/2 tablespoon chopped fresh mint
  • 1/2 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil
  • Pinch of ground cinnamon
  • Pinch of cayenne pepper
  • Salt and freshly ground pepper, to taste

Process

  1. Combine rice, chicken, carrots, scallions, and olives in a large bowl.
  2. Whisk together orange juice, lemon juice, mint, cilantro, oil, cinnamon, cayenne, and salt and pepper in another bowl to form dressing.
  3. Toss rice mixture with dressing.
