Southwestern Cheese Panini

A cheesy sandwich full of lots of vegetables and salsa that's perfect for lunch.

Ingredients

  • 1 ounce shredded sharp Cheddar cheese
  • 1/4 cup shredded zucchini
  • 1/8 cup shredded carrot
  • 1 tablespoon finely chopped red onion
  • 1 tablespoon prepared salsa
  • 1/4 tablespoon chopped pickled jalapeno pepper
  • 2 slices whole-wheat bread
  • 1/2 teaspoons canola oil

    • Process

    1. Combine Cheddar, zucchini, carrot, onion, salsa, and jalapeno in a medium bowl. Spread onto slice of bread and top with the remaining slice of bread.
    2. Heat canola oil in a large nonstick skillet over medium heat. Place the panini in the pan. Place a medium skillet on top of the panini to weigh it down. Cook the panini until golden on one side, about 2 minutes. Reduce the heat to medium- low, flip the panini, replace the top skillet, and cook until the second side is golden, 1 to 3 more minutes.
