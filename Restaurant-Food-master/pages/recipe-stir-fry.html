Szechuan Tofu and Green Bean Stir-Fry

A spicy vegetarian stir-fry.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons water
  • 1 tablespoon reduced-sodium soy sauce
  • 1/4 tablespoon tomato paste
  • 1/2 teaspoon Chinkiang vinegar or balsamic vinegar
  • 1/2 teaspoon sugar
  • Pinch of crushed red pepper
  • 1/2 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 4 ounces extra-firm tofu, drained
  • 1 cup green beans, trimmed and cut in half
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon minced fresh ginger

Process

  1. Whisk water, soy sauce, tomato paste, vinegar, sugar, crushed red pepper, and cornstarch in a small bowl. Cut tofu into 1/2 inch cubes and pat dry. Toss tofu in a bowl with cornstarch to coat.
  2. Heat oil in a wok or large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the tofu and spread out across the surface of the pan. Let cook for 2 minutes. Gently turn and stir. Continue cooking, stirring occassionally, until lightly browned and crispy. Transfer to a plate.
  3. Reduce heat to medium. Add the remaining oil to the pan. Add green beans, garlic, and ginger. Cook, stirring constantly, for 1 minute. Add the remaining water, cover and cook until the beans are crisp-tender. Stir the reserved soy sauce mixture and pour it over the green beans. cook, stirring until thickened. Add the tofu and cook, stirring until headed through, about 1 minute more.
