Jewel-Osco

 4042 W Foster Ave.

A regional grocery store and pharmacy chain that carries a wide variety of foods, but products that are not standard American fare will be more expensive. They always have hundreds of products on sale at any given time.

ALDI

 5001 N Pulaski Rd.

An international grocery store chain that carries basic American food staples almost exclusively. They do, however, have much lower prices than usual.

Sahar International Market

 4851 N Kedzie Ave.

A local grocery store that specializes in Middle Eastern foods and offers many halal foods.
